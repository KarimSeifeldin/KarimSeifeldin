Welcome to your quite place!

This the perfect place to escape from the busy city. Make yourself at home! Comfortable cabin in the middle of unspoiled private woods. Relax in the middle of natural beauty or visit the ridge or stream on one of our trails. Three miles of trails are for hiking in the summer and snowshoeing in the winter. Perfect place to gather with family and friends.

Activities on the property and near by

Roomy cabin is fully equipped and has a fireplace. Pets are welcome. Horseback riding is available nearby. Kayak Rental available for use on local lakes. Snowshoe Rental available. We are a 30 minute drive from Bangor, Maine or a 4 hour drive from Boston. Make sure you ask about the seasonal activities
